Could you make my adoption certificate a little bit bigger, squarer and more awkward looking please?

Perhaps it's the huge squareness of it, or the prospect of having the face of your MP's unflattering photo-booth-style face leering from your site for who knows how long. You told us with your non-publishing (and complaining. Fair enough!), so we made some more. Or rather, a cartoonist called Charlie Brooker did, when we asked him nicely. Thanks Charlie.
