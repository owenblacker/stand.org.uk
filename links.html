Useful UK Crypto Links

The Electronic Commerce Bill
The full text of the Bill.

European Directive
The European Commission has drafted a directive on digital signature law. If enacted, it would override any UK law to the extent that they conflict.

UKCrypto community
UKCrypto is the Internet mailing list for the professional cryptography community in the UK. Richard Clayton distilled the contents of months of discussion into a single document.

The Foundation for Information Policy Research (FIPR)
An independent organisation that studies the interaction between information technology and society

Campaign Against Censorship of the Internet in Britain (CACIB)
A campaigning group that covers privacy and censorship issues. Updated frequently with current developments.

Historical Links
These documents refer to previous revisions of the Bill and earlier policy proposals

DTI Statement April 1998
Outline of the DTI's plans as they stood in April 1998.

DTI Responses to consultation Exercise, Feb 1998
The DTI ran a consultation exercise from March 1997 to September 1997. This is their summary of the responses they received.

DTI Paper - Net Benefit
A general paper produced by the DTI stating that Electronic Commerce is important and beneficial.

'Cryptography and Democracy: Dilemmas of Freedom'
Yaman Akdeniz and Casper Bowden (of FIPR)'s excellent paper.
